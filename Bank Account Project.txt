Bank Account Simulator

A bank account has an owner, a balance, operations: deposit, withdraw, checkbalance

Important rules you should know
The amount you want to withdraw cannot be more than your balance
E.g 4000, when you have just 1000, can’t work

You cannot deposit a negative money
E.g Lohi deposits -20

Balance should update correctly


Case study:

Lohi deposits 500
Bal = 500

Lohi withdraw 200
Bal = 300

Lohi withdraw 1000
Bal = 300
output= Insufficient funds
